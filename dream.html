Readers dream is a website that intends to give user's access to their book of choice based on Genre and book type. users of the website will be able to acess their book of choice either through using the search bar, using filters, or both. People who have an account can access the reader dream session that will let them access a virtual audio book on the website that's narrated for them to listen to whenever they want. People who want to improve their reading skills can sign up for A premium that will give them the option to track how often they visit the site and track the effort they make.
